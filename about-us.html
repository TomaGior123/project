About Us

Our group- NEXUS A-6(NASA Exploration for Unique Solutions A-6) aims at developing a gripping, strategic and bonding game, designed to resolve the issue of playing games in a microgravity environment.

Our Mission

We aim to create unforgettable memories through engaging gameplay that fosters learning and collaboration. Our goal is to bring people together, transcending boundaries and distances, while having fun in an extraordinary environment.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe in providing an inclusive experience for everyone.
  • Innovation: We strive for innovation in gameplay and design.
  • Quality: Our commitment is to provide a high-quality gaming experience.
  • Community: Building a strong community of players is at the heart of what we do.
