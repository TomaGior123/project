Back To Earth

“Back to Earth” offers a unique experience for players of any age, anywhere, including space.
It fosters skills such as teamwork, communication, and promotes one’s critical thinking.
It is suited even for microgravity environments;
its compact and lightweight design makes it easy to handle and transport, allowing players to enjoy immersive gameplay no matter where they are in the cosmos.

RULES

• The general aim of this game is to fly back to the Earth.
• In order to build the rocket, a player needs to collect a certain amount of resources, such as Metal, Oxygen, and Water.
• Begin by spinning the spinner, then move your figure forward the number of spaces indicated.
• On whatever it lands on, you have to complete.
• The player that collects the required amount of resources to build the rocket wins.
